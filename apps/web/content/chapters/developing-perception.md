***SOLUTIONS FIFTEEN MATCHES TOPIC 04***

*If you take the 14^th^ match, you will win.*

*Alert players very soon realise that anyone faced with 5 matches left
cannot win. They take 1, you take 3. They take 2, you take 2. They take
3 and you take one, leaving them to take the 15th.*

*This means that if you take the 10^th^, then you should win. Most
players realise this quite early, and it gives the clue to work in fours
(14 -- 10 = 4)*

*By the same thinking 14 - 10 = 10 -- x. That 'gap' is 4.*

*So go for the 6^th^ match. 6 -- 4 = 2. So get that 2^nd^ match out, and
if you cannot,*

*then get the 6^th^ one out, that enables you to get the 10^th^, and
also the 14^th^.*

*You might, of course, bluff your opponent and become inventive.*

*Get the 2^nd^ Get the 6^th^. Get the 10^th^. Get the 14^th^ YOU WIN*
