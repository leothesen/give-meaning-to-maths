De Chardin drew the framework – as an Ultra-Futurist. Popper related it to the world of his day seeking a way of facing the new reality of man deciding his own future – as an ideal. And the consequence of all of this in giving man the ability to challenge and to take risks, allowed Quantum Theory to enter the Story of Man; seeking new problems and new possibilities.

Popper had proposed different models of a functioning new World model -- with the Scientific (Materialist) model gaining the mind of those developing the technology we see now.
